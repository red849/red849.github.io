World Percentantage of People with access to Electricity

This was my first group project where I programmed using MATLAB different parts of the world to light up based on their acces to electricity of the years. This was in correclation with the GDP growth over the years. In conclusion we saw that the countries with the highest GDP growth also had the highest percentage of access to electricity!

Water Valve

These are the final designs I made along with my group to create a water valve that would produce the most intake of water and deliver it to a container for testing. The goal of this project was to learn that you will never always succeed in your first attempt. Learn from your mistakes and you not only create a better product, but increase functionality significantly!
